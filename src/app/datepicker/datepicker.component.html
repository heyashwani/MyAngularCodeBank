Step1:"ng add ngx-bootstrap --component datepicker" Run this command. when you run this cmd your module.ts and angular.ts will autometically updated. In angular.ts stylesheet related to datepicker will be added.
Step2:Now add <input type="text" bsDatepicker /> in your html component. nothing to be added in it's ts file. Dont worry..
